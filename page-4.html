Alocasia Polly Moderate Bright indirect filtered light Water When 25-50% of the soil volume is dry. About once a week Prefers Temps between 65°-85°F with high humidity Fertilize every month during spring and summer Mildy toxic to humans and pets if ingested
Bird's Nest Fern Easy Medium to Bright Indirect Light Water every 1-2 weeks. Allow soil to fry out half way down between waterings. Thrive in temps between 60°- 80°F. Benefits from extra humidity Fertilize once a month during Growing season Pet Friendly
Calathea Rattlesnake Moderate Bright Indirect Light Water every 1 - 2 weeks, allowing soil to dry out half way down between waterings Thrives in warm temperatures, between 65°-85°F. Provide ample humidity. Fertilize once a month during spring and summer Pet Friendly. Non-toxic
Dracaena Lemon Lime Easy Medium Indirect Light Every 1 - 2 weeks, allowing soil to dry out between waterings. Prefers temps between 60°-75°F Fertilize once a month in spring and summer Not toxic to humans. Moderately toxic to pets.
Fiddle Leaf Fig Moderate to difficult Bright indirect light to full sun Every 1 - 2 weeks, allowing soil to dry out between waterings Room tempertures of 60°-80°F Fertilize once in the spring. Monthly during the summer. Toxic to human and pets if ingested
Golden Pothos Easy Low to bright indirect light Water every 1-2 weeks, allowing soil to dry out between waterings./td> Prefers temps between 65°-85°F Fertilize once a month in spring and summer Toxic to humans and pets if ingested
Monstera Deliciosa Easy Bright indirect to medium light Water every 1-2 weeks, allowing soil to dry out between waterings./td> Prefers temps between 60°-80°F Fertilize once a month in spring and summer Mildly toxic to humans and pets if ingested
Philodendron Heartleaf Easy Medium to bright indirect light Water every 1-2 weeks, allowing soil to dry out between waterings. Prefers temps between 65°-85°F Fertilize once a month in spring and summer Toxic to humans and pets if ingested
Snake Plant Easy Low to bright indirect light Water only when soil is dry Prefers a dry environment with temps 55°-85°F Fertilize once every 6 months Toxic to humans and pets if ingested
Stromanthe Moderate Medium to bright indirect light Every 1-2 weeks, allowing soil to dry out halfway between waterings Prefers temps between 65°-80°F, with ample humidity Fertilize once a month in spring and summer Pet friendly. Non-toxic.
